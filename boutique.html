JOSEPHINE JONES - BOUTIQUE

Josephine Jones Boutique is an appointment only designer vintage boutique, located in Bermondsey.

Founded as a home for Josephine Jones' extensive personal designer vintage collection, the boutique also offers photography studio facilities.

All enquiries related to visiting the boutique, utilising the studio facilities, or hires & purchases should be sent to miss.josephine.jones@gmail.com.
