JOSEPHINE JONES - BOUTIQUE

Josephine Jones Boutique is an eponymous womenswear brand founded by artist and model Josephine Jones, creating custom garments with an unapologetic embrace of femininity in prints, bias cuts and silhouettes.

Josephine Jones Boutique operates from a multidiciplinary perspective, blurring the line between art and fashion. Playing with perceptions of glamour in the creation of one-of-a-kind garments, styling of shoots and hosting of events.

In August 2018 Josephine Jones Boutique launched its physical premises in Bermondsey functioning as a design and photography studio, as well as a home for Josephine's personal designer vintage collection.

Josephine Jones Boutique is appointment only - bookings and enquiries should be sent to miss.josephine.jones@gmail.com.
